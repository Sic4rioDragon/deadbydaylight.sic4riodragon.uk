Introduction

How I usually play

I made this rulebook for fun and to show the kind of match experience I enjoy. It is not a contract, not a promise of mercy, and not a guarantee that I will play nice just because someone else does.

I’m at around 700 hours now, so I’m not writing this from a brand-new player point of view anymore. I still like learning, improving, and having memorable matches more than just making everything sweaty for no reason.

On killer, Huntress and Bubba are my mains. I also like playing Nurse, Clown, The Lich, Artist, Onryō, and Ghost Face.

On survivor, I mostly just play Yui. Sometimes I swap to Dino Nugget or Orela when I’m pairing with friends, but survivor is very much not the side I focus on most.

TLDR

The short version

Killer

  • I am the killer. I usually play to win.
  • Being nice does not guarantee safety.
  • If you BM me, I usually match the energy.
  • If you play normally and respectfully, I notice that too.
  • Huntress and Bubba are my mains.
  • I like fun pressure, clean hits, and satisfying moments.

Survivor

  • I mostly play Yui.
  • I am not the survivor bully type.
  • I usually just want a chill match, decent teamwork, and some improvement.
  • I am more likely to help than troll.
  • If the killer is cool, I appreciate it.

Killer Rules

What I respect and what gets punished

General behavior

  • If you play normal and don’t BM, I usually keep things normal too.
  • If you spam teabags or click at me nonstop, I’m more likely to focus you.
  • If you sandbag your teammate on purpose, that gets noticed fast.
  • If you’re clearly just being rude for the sake of it, I don’t mind punishing that.
  • If you’re friendly in a genuine way, I often match that energy depending on the match.

Hooks, tunneling, slugging

  • I do not go into matches planning to face-camp for no reason.
  • I do not usually tunnel unless the match flow or your own actions keep putting you back in front of me.
  • If you use off-hook protection to bodyblock me on purpose, I will hit you.
  • I try not to over-slug unless the survivor team is forcing that kind of game with saves or pressure.
  • Endgame pressure is endgame pressure. I am not going to pretend I’m suddenly not the killer anymore.

Match flow

  • If gens are flying, I play harder.
  • If the match turns goofy and everyone is being chill, I sometimes lean into that instead.
  • If I hard win, I sometimes let one person go.
  • If the whole match feels miserable or awkward, I might mentally check out and just finish it out.
  • I like matches that feel active and honest more than matches where everyone hides forever.

My killer taste

Huntress is still the one I enjoy most because nothing beats a clean hatchet. Bubba is the other real main for me because he’s simple, brutal, and fun in a very different way. Outside of those, I like Nurse, Clown, Artist, Onryō, Ghost Face, and The Lich when I’m in the mood for them.

Survivor Rules

How I am on the other side

Survivor is not my main focus, so I mostly keep it simple. I usually play Yui, and sometimes switch to Dino Nugget or Orela if I’m matching with friends.

  • I’m usually trying to do gens, help teammates, and not be dead weight.
  • I’m not trying to bully the killer.
  • I don’t really care about being flashy just to be annoying.
  • If the killer gives hatch or opens gate, I appreciate it.
  • If the killer is toxic first, I can match the energy back.
  • Mostly I just want a match that feels decent and not stupid.

Mindset

What this page is really for

This page is less about trying to force rules onto random people and more about showing my playstyle. I like fun matches, I like learning, and I like moments that feel memorable.

I do not expect perfect survivors, perfect killers, or perfect etiquette every match. I just prefer people who don’t go out of their way to make the game miserable.

If you are respectful, I usually notice. If you are annoying on purpose, I usually notice that too.
